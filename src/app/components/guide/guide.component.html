Schwan lake - Fússen - Germany

Around The World

Schwan Lake - Germany

Lake Schwan (German Schwansee, meaning "swan lake") is an alpine lake in Bavaria in the district of East Allgäu, Schwangau southeast of Füssen, near the castles Hohenschwangau and Neuschwanstein, 400 meters north of the lake Alpsee and 789.23 meters above sea level. . In the 19th century, the kings of the Wittelsbach family included it in a park designed by the landscape designer Peter Joseph Lenné. Since 1956 it has been part of a nature reserve. The lake serves as a spa during the summer, while in winter it is used as a skating rink for locals and tourists.
